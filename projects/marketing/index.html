Graphic Design: Marketing Materials

A collection of flyers and related media assets for advertising.

Project Overview

My Passion: Graphic design is my passion and I have always enjoyed making digital materials such as flyers, banners, and other creative works. In many endeavors, I created flyers to advertise my own events or to collaborate with other organizations.

My Tools: I primarily use Adobe Photoshop to make detailed flyers. For uniform designs requiring specific assets or quick outputs, I utilize Canva.

My Design Principles: High Resolution, Accessibility, Readability, Vibrancy, Engagement
