Florida Collegiate Gaming Convention

I created a free-to-attend convention that unified Florida's fragmented gaming community.

Project Overview

The Organization: Florida Game Con is an anime, gaming, and tabletop convention focused on recreational and competitive gaming. The convention provides the Florida community with pop culture activities, learning opportunities, culture awareness, and social connection.

My Role: I founded and coordinated the convention and all related activities virtually and in-person.

My Efforts: My convention uplifted local businesses, promoted higher education to K-12 students, recruited international talent, and partnered with major companies such as Microsoft and Nintendo.

The Challenge

Florida had fragmented gaming initiatives, limited esports support, and few inclusive pop culture events.

My Approach

Made the event free and open to all skill levels; relied on sponsors and vendors to sustain it.

The Outcome

A recurring, hybrid-accessible convention blending gaming, anime, and tech culture—accessible and student-powered.
