Universal Design for Learning Tools

I created a repository webpage to provide educators with resources that utilize universal design principles for learning.

Project Overview

The Project: A collection of instructional tools that represent Universal Design for Learners (UDL) practices. The software showcased is free to use forever or offer a free-trial capability.

The Audience: Teachers who need assistance in creating UDL-based curricula and free technology resources.

My Focus: Showcasing Innovation Solutions to Promote Accessible Education

The Challenge

Educational institutions lack the funding to supply teachers with UDL-based software or educators are not aware of resources that exist.

My Approach

Create a digital hub that showcases numerous technologies. My approach is multifaceted; I showcase the tools while also making the repository a learning example through my use of Sutori.

The Outcome

Educators can easily find and utilize free resources based on their needs, the UDL principles they satisfy, and software features.
