Project Overview

I served the students of University of South Florida (USF) as the president of the Video Game Club for three years. My accomplishments included recruiting 1000 active members, organizing 100+ events, generating a $10,000+ budget, and overseeing member training.

The Challenge

The needs of students were unmet due to lack of support for certain gaming opportunities. Conflict occured between recreational and competitive gaming crowds.

★
My Approach

In response to feedback, utilized a student-first approach of surveys, community engagement, new activites, and increased advertising. Events engaged gamers of all interests, in response to feedback via free play events and esports teams.

✪
The Outcome

The organization grew into the largest student organization on campus. I created a balance of recreational and competitive gaming. The organization continually adapted functions to make an inclusive space for all students to participate.
